About Ghana

Ghana, known as the "Gateway to Africa," is famous for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning beaches. From the bustling markets of Accra to the historic Cape Coast Castle, there's something for everyone.

  • Capital: Accra
  • Population: ~33 million
  • Languages: English, Twi, Ewe
  • Currency: Ghanaian Cedi (GHS)
