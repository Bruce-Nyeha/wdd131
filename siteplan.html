SolLichtRise Tech – Site Planning Document

Prepared by Bruce Nyeha | WDD 131 – Week 05

1. Site Name

sollichtrise.org

“Sol” (Sun) + “Licht” (Light in German) + “Rise” = Sun Light Rise.
The name represents renewable energy, innovation, and the rise of African technology. The logo is already designed and will be the main brand identity across all platforms.

2. Site Purpose

SolLichtRise Tech exists to accelerate Ghana’s and Africa’s digital and energy transformation by:

  • Providing cutting-edge solar & renewable energy solutions
  • Developing IoT devices tailored for African markets
  • Training and employing young African software engineers
  • Building world-class software products from Ghana to the world

The website will serve as the official face of the company, showcase projects, attract talent, and generate leads.

3. Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  • What are the most efficient solar panels for Ghana’s climate?
  • How can I join the SolLichtRise Tech engineering team?
  • Where is SolLichtRise Tech located?
  • How can I contact the team or request a quote?
  • What software products has SolLichtRise built?

4. Color Schema

#D32F2F Primary Red – Headers, Navigation, Buttons
#FFFFFF White – Main background
#212121 Dark Gray – Body text
#0288D1 Light Blue – Footer, accents

5. Typography

Orbitron – Headings & Logo (Bold, futuristic, tech feel)

Inter – Body text (Clean, highly readable sans-serif)
